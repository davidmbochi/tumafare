We Are Tumafare

At Tumafare, we understand the risks involved in sending money for services and not receiving what was promised. Our mission is to eliminate these risks by acting as a secure intermediary between the sender and receiver. With Tumafare, when you send money, it is held safely in our escrow account until you confirm that the service has been delivered to your satisfaction. Only then is the money released to the receiver. When the money is sent, the client also receives a notification for the same.

We utilize M-pesa for our transactions, ensuring a seamless and reliable payment process. Our service fee is 5%, exclusive of the standard transaction charges. Tumafare brings confidence and trust to your financial dealings, providing peace of mind for both parties involved
